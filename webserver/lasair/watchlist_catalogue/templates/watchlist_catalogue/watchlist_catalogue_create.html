Create a New Catalogue Watchlists

A Catalogue Watchlist is composed of a set of locations in the sky, together with an association radius in arcseconds. These locations are presumably coordinates of sources of specific interest to you. Use a Catalogue Watchlist to be alerted of transient events found in association with any of these sources. Your Catalogue Watchlists will appear in the My Catalogue Watchlists of the Catalogue Watchlists index.

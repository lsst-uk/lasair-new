How to use this form

The following details the syntax for constraint qualification, already used by major astronomical querying engines, including Vizier. The qualification is made of comparison operators, logical operators, and values. The ways of specifying these different components differ slightly for numeric, character, or other types of data. Don'tforget that the special symbols have to be converted when embedded in URL syntax (e.g. %25 for the % sign, %3C for <, etc.)

OperatorMeaningExample

|OR (logical operator)1..5 | 7..9
